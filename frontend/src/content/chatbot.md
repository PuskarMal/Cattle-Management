## FAQ

Q: What is SAMRIDHI?
A: It is a dual-purpose digital platform designed to manage livestock data and facilitate commerce. It serves as a database for storing animal health records and an e-commerce marketplace for selling veterinary medicines and cattle feed.

Q: How to register animal details?
A: Users must input the animal's specific details—including name and location—and attach a photo to successfully save the record in the system.

Q: What is a marketplace?
A: The marketplace is the commercial hub of the application. It connects farmers with suppliers, allowing them to buy and sell essential resources such as medicines, animal feed, and breeding services.

Q: How to upload your cow/buffalo photo?
A: Clicking the upload button, users can browse their phone’s gallery, select the specific image of their cattle, and upload it to the platform.

Q: How do I change the language?
A: To ensure accessibility for all users, this feature allows customization of the interface. Users can select their preferred regional language from a list, instantly translating the app's text for better understanding.

Q: What is my profile?
A: This is the user's personal dashboard. It acts as a central repository for the account holder's private information, contact details, and other relevant user-specific data saved within the application.
